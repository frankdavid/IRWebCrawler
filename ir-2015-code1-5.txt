Methodology:
We process the content within elements matching #contentMain and .textList. If the amount of text retrieved this way is
not sufficient, we consider the whole textual content on the page.

By default the crawler disregards login pages. If you want them included, provide the `withlogin` flag after the url,
like this:
java -jar ir-2015-code1-5.jar http://idvm-infk-hofmann03.inf.ethz.ch/eth/www.ethz.ch/en.html withlogin
